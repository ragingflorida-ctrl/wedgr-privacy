Privacy Policy

App: WEDGR  |  Package: com.wedgr.app  |  Last updated: April 1, 2025

Short version: WEDGR does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the app stores stays on your device and is fully under your control.

1. Who We Are

WEDGR is an Android application developed and operated as a sole proprietorship. For privacy-related questions, contact us at: raging.florida@gmail.com

2. What WEDGR Does

WEDGR turns your Android device into a wireless barcode scanner wedge. It scans barcodes, QR codes, and text via the device camera and transmits the results to a paired Windows or macOS computer over Bluetooth HID (keyboard emulation). The app functions like a hardware barcode scanner connected to a PC.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account or identity information
  • No location data
  • No analytics or usage statistics
  • No crash reports sent to any server
  • No photos or images — camera captures are processed on-device and immediately discarded; only the extracted text or barcode value is retained
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data is transmitted to WEDGR, Anthropic, or any third party

4. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

WEDGR stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Scan history — barcode values, QR code content, and OCR text you capture, saved to a CSV file in the app's private storage directory
  • Paired device names and Bluetooth MAC addresses — the names and addresses of host computers you have paired with WEDGR, stored in the app's private SharedPreferences
  • Last-seen timestamps — the date and time each paired device was last connected

None of this data leaves your device. It is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted to any server.

5. How to Delete Your Data

You have full control over all locally stored data:

  • Scan history: Tap Settings → Clear All Data inside the app, or uninstall the app
  • Paired devices: Tap Settings → Manage Devices → Forget for each device, or uninstall the app
  • All data: Uninstalling WEDGR permanently removes all locally stored data

6. Permissions Used

  • Camera — required to scan barcodes, QR codes, and capture text. Images are processed on-device and never stored or transmitted.
  • Bluetooth (BLUETOOTH_CONNECT, BLUETOOTH_ADVERTISE, BLUETOOTH_SCAN) — required to discover, pair with, and transmit keystrokes to a host computer via Bluetooth HID. Bluetooth scanning is never used to derive location.
  • Foreground Service / Connected Device — required to maintain an active Bluetooth HID connection while the app is in use.
  • Vibration — used for optional haptic feedback on successful scans.
  • Internet — declared as a standard React Native dependency; WEDGR makes no network requests.

7. Third-Party SDKs

WEDGR uses the following on-device libraries. None of these transmit data off your device:

  • Google ML Kit Text Recognition — on-device OCR processing only
  • Expo / React Native — app framework; no telemetry in release builds

8. Children's Privacy

WEDGR is a productivity tool intended for use in business and professional environments. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
